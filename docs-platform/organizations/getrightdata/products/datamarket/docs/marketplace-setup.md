## Branding
- Upload logo variations (light/dark, square) under **Branding**.
- Choose accent colors to match your primary brand (hex or RGB).

## Domains
Map a custom domain such as `exchange.acme.com` via CNAME. TLS managed by GetRightData or your own certificate (upload via PEM bundle).

## Billing
- Native billing integrates with Stripe.
- Provide tax information and payout schedule.
- Toggle trial pricing or credit-based billing.

## Navigation
Configure top navigation categories (Solutions, Industries, Data Products). You can link back into docs for contextual help.
